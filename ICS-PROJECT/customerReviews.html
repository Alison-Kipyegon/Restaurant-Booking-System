My visit to The Global Gourmet was a mix of highs and lows. The decor is charming, with a rustic Italian feel that sets the mood beautifully. The bruschetta we ordered as an appetizer was delicious, with a perfect balance of tomatoes, basil, and balsamic. For the main course, I had the spaghetti carbonara, which was creamy and flavorful, though a tad too salty for my taste. The service was attentive, but we had to wait a bit longer than expected for our food. Overall, a lovely place with great potential, but a few minor tweaks could make it even better
